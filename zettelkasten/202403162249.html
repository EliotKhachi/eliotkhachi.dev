AWS Machine Learning and AI Services

  • Amazon SageMaker - build, train, and deploy ML models for any use case with a fully managed infrastructure.

  • Amazon Textract- automatically extract text and data from scanned documents.

  • Amazon Rekognition - Add image and video analysis, i.e. objective detection and facial search, to your applications.

  • Amazon Polly - Turn text into lifelike speech.

  • Amazon Transcribe - Turn speech into text.

  • Amazon Kendra - Intelligent search through websites and documents.
