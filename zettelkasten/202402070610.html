Production Rules

A production rule, or production, is a rewrite rule that specifies a symbol substitution. Each rule has a head, or left-hand side, which consists of the string that can be replaced, and a body, or right hand-side, which consists of the string that may replace it. The head must be a NonTerminal Symbols, while the body may consist of a combination of Terminal Symbols and nonterminal symbols and/or terminal strings.
