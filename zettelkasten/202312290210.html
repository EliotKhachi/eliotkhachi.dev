MBSE Background

  • What is Model-Based Systems Engineering (MBSE) and what value does it provide? [What is Model-Based Systems Engineering? ]
  • A "model" is a digital representation of a system [What is a System]. In the backend, the model is stored in a NoSQL database.
  • The model is not shown all at once. Rather, there are various "views" of the model. Each view is textually encoded as an XML/JSON/SysML file, with a block-diagram-like graphical representation. The graphical and textual representations of SysML are much more expressive than block diagrams [What is SysML?].
