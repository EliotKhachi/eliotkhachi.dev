Sunk Costs

Sunk costs are costs that have already been spent and cannot be recovered. This is to be contrasted from future costs that a business may face, which should be factored when making business decisions. Sunk costs, on the other hand, shouldn't be considered. [Sunk Cost Fallacy]

Examples

  • salaries
  • insurance
  • rent
  • non-refundable deposits or repairs
